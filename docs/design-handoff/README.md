# VitaOS — Design Handoff

A complete design handoff package for the UI/UX/CX team taking VitaOS from a working scaffold to a beautiful product.

---

## Read in this order

For a designer landing here for the first time:

1. **[00 — The Story](./00-the-story.md)** — Why this exists. The narrative. The stakes. *Read first. Get inspired.*
2. **[01 — The Product](./01-the-product.md)** — What VitaOS actually is, in detail. Concepts, models, surfaces.
3. **[02 — The People](./02-the-people.md)** — The five personas you're designing for, with names and stories.
4. **[03 — The Journeys](./03-the-journeys.md)** — Real moments in real time. The narrative walkthroughs.
5. **[04 — The Screens](./04-the-screens.md)** — Page-by-page requirements. What's on each screen and what users do there.
6. **[05 — The Components](./05-the-components.md)** — The reusable inventory. What to build into the design system.
7. **[06 — The Content](./06-the-content.md)** — Real example copy: briefings, conversations, error states.
8. **[07 — The Market](./07-the-market.md)** — Business context. Customers, pricing, competition, positioning.
9. **[08 — The Mood](./08-the-mood.md)** — Visual references, quality bar, anti-patterns.

There's also the original [MMDD design brief](../MMDD-vitaos-design-brief.md) — a tighter overview if you want the executive summary version.

---

## What we want from you

A complete design system + a beautiful product across three apps:

- **VitaOS Operator App** — desktop-first workspace control center
- **Tom App** — personal AI co-pilot PWA (mobile-first)
- **Tim App** — team AI co-pilot PWA

Plus the marketing/docs surfaces, plus the white-label experience.

---

## What we'll give you

- A working engineering scaffold (already built — 40+ packages, 34 routes, full data flow)
- Engineering pairing on every page
- Total authority on the visual direction
- Freedom to push back on the current IA, structure, and naming

---

## What we won't compromise on

- The autonomy model (L1-L4) and how it's expressed must be perfect
- Lineage must be visible and trustworthy at every step
- The product must work for Marcus, Linda, and Daniel — all three
- Empty states must feel inviting, not embarrassing
- The white-label experience must be a *moat*, not a feature

---

## Where to start

1. Read the docs in order (above)
2. Look at the current scaffold (`apps/operator-app`, `apps/tom-app`, `apps/tim-app`) to understand what's already there — and feel free to throw most of it out
3. Schedule a kickoff with engineering — bring questions, especially on the open ones in [08 — The Mood §8.5](./08-the-mood.md#85-the-agent-presence-question)
4. Start with the autonomy switcher + approval card (the two most-important moments)
